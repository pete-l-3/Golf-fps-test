Step 1 of 4

Set up the shot

Position the phone behind her, then confirm the club for this session.

  • Mount the phone sideways (landscape) on the tripod — it captures the widest view of the swing and flight
  • Stand the phone behind her, roughly hip height
  • Mark a 1'×1' square on the ground (tape, chalk, a hoop — whatever's handy) where the ball goes. It's mainly for her own consistency between shots — the box in the preview just shows how much wiggle room the camera can still track within
  • Try ~6–8 ft back to start, then adjust until the ball placement box frames comfortably in view
  • This preview is just for lining things up — actual recording happens in Camera, not here
